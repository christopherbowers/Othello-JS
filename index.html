Othello

How to Win

The object of the game is to have the majority of your colour discs on the board at the end of the game.

How to Play

Black plays first. Click on a valid spot on the grid. You must place a disc on a spot that allows you to “flank” or “capture” at least one of your opponent’s discs by bordering them in a row.

All captured discs will flip to your color.

To capture your opponent’s discs you must have 1 of your own pieces at the end of a row and then on your turn place a new piece at the beginning of the row. Everything in between on that row is now flipped to your color. You can only capture rows of a single color adjacent to each other; there cannot be any open space or your own discs between them or the combo is interrupted.

You can flank any number of discs. You may capture discs vertically, or horizontally. You may capture in multiple directions at the same time.

If you cannot make a valid move then your turn is forfeited and your opponent may go again. If you have a valid move available to you then you must make that move and are not allowed to forfeit your turn.

When neither player can make any further play then the game is over.
